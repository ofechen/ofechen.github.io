How To Keep Nuni Alive and (Mildly) Happy

What When What else?
Food Every morning and every evening The bowl is in the hallway, her food is in the pantry (right in front of it) in a plastic box. The cat food bag is right next to it. We give her one scoop twice a day. She sometimes won't finish it, don't worry about it.
Water Whenever / Every 2-3 days She has two bowls, one next to her food, the other next to the garbage can/fridge. Make sure they are not empty and keep in mind that she likes fresh water so if you really want to make her happy, wash the bowl and replace the water every 2-3 days.
Litter Box Every 2-3 days Scoop whatever needs scooping into one of the plastic bags next to the box. the scooper is right there as well. If you feel she could use some additional litter, there are plastic bags near by on the floor.
Play Time and Treats Every night (optional) We usually give Nuni treats every night at around 8 or 9 pm. She might even expect it from you :). But, we also try to make her for them. We usually play with her with the feather-on-a-string toy for 10 to 20 minutes before giving her about 8 treats. You can find the treats on top of the fridge. The toys are either on the floor in the living room or under the TV. We rotate them every once in a while to keep things interesting for her :)
Hair Whenever We have a vacuum in the closet in the back office. We use it once or twice a week since Nuni sheds a lot of hair during the summer. Feel free to use it if the hair bothers you. There’s also a roller for hair that you can use for your clothes in the coat closet in front of the fridge.

If you run out of cat food, litter, treats or anything else, please order them on Amazon and we'll pay you back. Links are embedded in the table.
We will mostly be available, feel free to call us with anything urgent or text us with questions.
